Frequently Asked Questions

Is Video Converter Pro really free?

Yes, completely free and open source (MIT license). No watermarks, no file limits, no signup. Both the desktop app and online version are free forever.

Do my files get uploaded to a server?

No. Everything is processed locally on your device. The online version uses WebAssembly to convert files directly in your browser. Your videos never leave your device — 100% private.

Does it work on iPhone and Android?

Yes! The online version works on any device with a modern browser — iPhone, iPad, Android, Windows, Mac, Linux. You can install it as an app on your home screen too.

What video formats are supported?

20+ formats: MP4, AVI, MKV, MOV, WebM, WMV, FLV, GIF, TS, 3GP for video. MP3, AAC, WAV, OGG, M4A for audio extraction.

How do I convert a video to MP4?

Drag and drop your video, select MP4 as output, choose a quality preset, click Convert. The file downloads automatically. No signup needed.

What's the difference between Desktop and Online?

The desktop version (Windows) uses native FFmpeg for maximum speed and no file size limits. The online version uses WebAssembly — slightly slower but works on any device including phones and tablets.
